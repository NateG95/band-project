Bon Jovi is an American rock band formed in the early 1980's. The band currently consists of vocalist Jon Bon Jovi, keyboardist David Bryan, drummer Tico Torres, guitarist Phil X, and bassist Hugh McDonald. Their debut album, self-titled "Bon-Jovi" was released on January 21, 1984. The "Bon-Jovi" album put them on the spotlight, but it was not until the release of their third album "Slippery When Wet" did they achieve widespread success and global recognition.
